CircuitDraw v0.4

Controls Help

  • Click and drag to draw components.
  • While drawing, you may enter numerical parameters to the component you're drawing (eg: 1.33k, 6.5m).
  • While drawing, press ESC to cancel current component
  • Release the mouse, or click again, or pressing Enter will draw the component
  • Use Scroll Wheel to cycle through different componenents you want to draw:
    Passive Active Others
    • Wire
    • Resistor
    • Capacitor
    • Electrolytic Capacitor
    • Inductor
    • Diode
    • Voltage Source
    • Current Source
    • Battery
    • Operational Amplifier
    • Oscillator
    • Voltage reference
    • Ground
    • Antenna
    • IC
  • Use Middle mouse button to toggle between Draw mode and Edit mode.
  • In Edit mode, mouse over an existing component to highlight it, click once to select it. Click again to deselect. You may also click and drag to select multiple components.
  • Press delete to delete the selected components.
  • Press ESC to deselect.
  • Press G to toggle the grid.

Saving/Loading Help

  • Click on the title on the top to change name of the current circuit. You can enter anything you want (eg: H-Bridge). Press Enter to save the name.
  • Click "Save" and you will receive a download of the current circuit as a .json file.
  • To load a circuit, choose the .json file to upload and click "Load". Warning: Loading will discard all components on the screen and then reload
